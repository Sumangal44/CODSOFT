Albert Einstein

The Genius Who Revolutionized Physics

Albert Einstein (1879-1955) was a renowned theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics.

Key Achievements

  • Developed the theory of relativity
  • Contributed to the development of quantum mechanics
  • Received the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921
  • Published more than 300 scientific papers

Famous Quotes

"Imagination is more important than knowledge. Knowledge is limited. Imagination encircles the world."
"The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existing."
